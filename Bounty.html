Bounty Info



Our simple and useful bounty program was created for all our users to help us spread CryptoNumismat around the world. As our "thanks" to you three the most active users receive New Notes at the starting price of 0.5 ETH.

How to participate?


You can use any social media on your choice. Do as much as you wish and send an email to cryptonumismat@gmail.com with screenshots of your effort and your Ethereum wallet address. Your contributions will be estimated by the effect of those efforts.

To increase your chances we propose you to place all your sharing efforts into one email for that we could estimate your contribution correctly.

Winners are selected and announced at the end of spring.
The results will be placed on our Discord channel.
